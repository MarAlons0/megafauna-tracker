Megafauna Tracker — Help

Track large mammal sightings across North America using verified iNaturalist observations, with AI-powered wildlife briefings for any location.

How to use

  1. Tap Filters to open the controls bar
  2. Search a location or choose a Quick Pick
  3. Adjust radius, time window, and species groups as needed
  4. Tap Load — sightings appear on the map and in the feed
  5. Tap Analysis for an AI-generated wildlife briefing and chat

Tips

  • Tap My Location for the fastest start — sightings load automatically
  • Tap a species group label (e.g. Bears) to drill into per-species colors
  • Use the fullscreen button on the map for a bigger view
